La page des kits — espace d'achat clair et festif permettant de sélectionner et commander son kit de participation Poro Run.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Platform: Web, Mobile-first responsive
- Theme: Dark, festive, vibrant — deep night carnival energy
- Background: Cosmic Midnight Navy (#0a0a23) with radial orange/pink/violet atmospheric glows
- Primary Accent: Molten Festival Orange (#ff7d00) for primary buttons and hover states
- Secondary Accent: Hot Carnival Pink (#ff4da6) for gradients, price highlights, links
- Tertiary Accent: Electric Dream Violet (#7b4cff) for gradient blends
- Text Primary: Frosted White (#ffffff) on all dark surfaces
- Text Secondary: Whisper Gray (#6b7280) for item descriptions
- Card Surfaces: Glass-dark (rgba(255,255,255,0.04))
- Typography: Poppins (Bold 700 headings, SemiBold 600 sub-headings, Regular 400 body)
- Buttons: 6px rounded, orange-pink gradient "ACHETER" CTA; outline style for quantity controls
- Layout: Product card grid (3-col desktop → 1-col mobile), cart recap block below

**Page Structure:**
1. **Page Title Section:** Centered heading "Acheter Votre Kit" + subtext "Choisissez le kit qui vous convient pour Poro Run"
2. **Product Card Grid:** One card per kit — product photo, kit name (bold), price in FCFA (bold pink), benefits list (bullet points), quantity +/− selector, "ACHETER" CTA button
3. **Cart Recap Block:** Summary card below the product grid — "RÉCAPITULATIF" heading, line items with quantities and totals, total price display, "VALIDER LES ACHATS" and "VIDER LE PANIER" action buttons side-by-side
4. **Footer:** Same as global footer
