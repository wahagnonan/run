La page d'accueil festive et énergique de Poro Run — le festival sportif et culturel de Korhogo.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Platform: Web, Mobile-first responsive
- Theme: Dark, festive, vibrant — deep night carnival energy
- Background: Cosmic Midnight Navy (#0a0a23) with radial orange/pink/violet atmospheric glows
- Primary Accent: Molten Festival Orange (#ff7d00) for primary buttons and hover states
- Secondary Accent: Hot Carnival Pink (#ff4da6) for gradients, links, icon fills
- Tertiary Accent: Electric Dream Violet (#7b4cff) for gradient blends and icon accents
- Text Primary: Frosted White (#ffffff) on all dark surfaces
- Text Secondary: Whisper Gray (#6b7280) for subtitles and muted copy
- Card Surfaces: Glass-dark (rgba(255,255,255,0.04))
- Typography: Poppins (Bold 700 headings, SemiBold 600 sub-headings, Regular 400 body)
- Buttons: 6px rounded, orange-to-pink gradient for primary CTAs, white text
- Navbar: Sticky dark gradient, blurs on scroll
- Layout: Flexbox-first, 2-column desktop, stacked mobile

**Page Structure:**
1. **Sticky Navbar:** "Poro Run" logo left, navigation links right (Accueil · Événements · Tickets · À propos), hamburger menu on mobile
2. **Hero Section:** Bold heading "PORO RUN" + "Festival" sub-label left, photo carousel of race participants right — primary CTA button "Acheter un kit" in orange-pink gradient
3. **Activities Section:** 3-column card row (Ambiance · Endurance · Rencontre et fun) with icon or image per card
4. **Gallery Section:** "Découvrez l'ambiance" — 3 atmospheric photos in a responsive grid
5. **Kit Teaser:** Side-by-side layout — kit photo carousel left, "Voir les kits" CTA button right
6. **Location / GPS Section:** Event date & hours display, embedded Google Maps iframe, two action buttons (Directions · Use my location)
7. **Partners/Sponsors Section:** "Nos Partenaires" — sponsor logo grid with names and tier labels
8. **Footer:** 3-column — Contact (email, phone, address) · Copyright · Social links (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, WhatsApp)
